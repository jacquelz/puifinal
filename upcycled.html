Upcycled Growth Using IoT



Project Overview


Problem Space:

Upcycling domestic possessions could offer households greater discretion and control over how computing capabilities offered by IoT are woven into everyday life by supporting tailoring those IoT objects to the home.


Process:

1. Review existing literature on the upcycled home

2. Analyze data collected over a 7 day study to characterize upcycling needs and opportunities in the home using Atlas.ti

3. Summarize findings through paper writing for upcycling common objects through computing


Research Findings






Outcomes

Investigated IoT applications with visual data from 10 households through coding groups and analytical memoing in Atlas.ti. Evaluated needs and opportunities in upcycling everyday objects with computing capabilities to synthesize findings in research paper.
