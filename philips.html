Designing for Engagement



Project Overview


Problem Space:

To derive a set of design guidelines and recommendations for how to design for engagement.


Process:

1. Review literature and research apps to understand problem space and collect design recommendations

2. Synthesize data and discover trends about sleep tracker engagement

3. Identify factors for re-engagement


Research Findings






Outcomes

Derived design guidelines and recommendations for Philips Respironics by reviewing literature and current products details. Explored user experience implications with gamification, personal informatics, and customization for long-term engagement.
